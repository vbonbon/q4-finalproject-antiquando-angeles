Keep these abbrieviations in mind!

ss - slip stitch
sc - single crochet

increase - 2 sc in the same stitch

fake magic ring - make a slip knot chain 3, ss into the 1st chain, chain, then insert round 1 into the circle formed between the chains OR make a regular magic ring

Step 1: Reference Selection

Go through to the Marketplace to select one of our many amazing items.

Step 2: Form the Head


Round 1 : 8 sc

Round 2 : 2 sc in every stitch (16)

Round 3 : 1 sc, increase (24)

Round 4 : 2 sc, increase (32)

Round 5 : 3 sc, increase (40)

Round 6 to 13 : 1 sc in every stitch (40 in each round)

Round 14 : 3 sc, decrease (32)

Round 15 : 2 sc, decrease (24)

Round 16 : 1 sc, decrease (16)

Round 17 : 8 decreases

Face (embroider the nose then sew onto the head)

Round 1 : 8 sc

Round 2 : 2 sc in every stitch (16)

Step 3: Form the Hat


Round 1 : 8 sc

Round 2 : 2 sc in every stitch (16)

Round 3 : 1 sc, increase (24)

Round 4 : 2 sc, increase (32)

Round 5 : 3 sc, increase (40)

Round 6 - 4 sc, increase (48)

Round 7 to 13 - 1 sc in every stitch (48 in each round)

Round 14 - 3 sc, decrease (you can also do *4 sc, decrease* but I prefer *3 sc, decrease* because it's tighter around the head)

Round 15 - 1 sc in every stitch (I didn't keep track of stitch count here)

Step 4: Form the Arms


Round 1 - 6 sc

Round 2 - 2 sc in every stitch (12)

Round 3 to 8 - 1 sc in every stitch (12)

Round 9 - 6 decreases

Round 10 - 1 sc in every stitch (6)

Step 5: Form the Legs


Round 1 - 8 sc

Round 2 - 2 sc in every stitch (16)

Round 3 to 5 - 1 sc in every stitch (16)

Step 6: Form the Ears


Round 1 - 8 sc

Round 2 - 2 sc in every stitch (16)

Round 3 - 1 sc in every stitch (16)

Step 7: Form the Cherries and Bows


Round 1 - 6 sc

Round 2 - 1 sc, increase (9)

Round 3 - 1 sc, decrease (6)

Decrease then ss to close the hole and fasten off

Make a slip knot and chain 70+ then fasten off to make the stem

Attach the cherries onto the ends of the stem and tie it into a bow around the bear
